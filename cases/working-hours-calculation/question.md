# The Question

## Original Question (Chinese)

公司实行实现每天8小时工作制,月计薪天数取21.75天,带薪年假10天/年。我今年总计实际出勤时间为2000小时,另外休了5天带薪年假。全部换算成小时计算话,考虑带薪年假为必休时长的情况下,是我欠公司的工时,还是公司欠我工时?具体是多少小时?

## English Translation

The company implements an 8-hour workday system, with a monthly payroll calculation base of 21.75 days and 10 days of paid annual leave per year. This year, my total actual attendance time was 2000 hours, and I took 5 days of paid leave. Converting everything to hours, and considering paid annual leave as mandatory rest time, do I owe the company working hours, or does the company owe me working hours? Specifically, how many hours?

## Key Parameters

- **Daily working hours**: 8 hours/day
- **Monthly payroll base**: 21.75 days/month
- **Annual paid leave**: 10 days/year
- **Actual attendance**: 2000 hours
- **Paid leave taken**: 5 days (40 hours)
- **Paid leave not taken**: 5 days (40 hours)

## Expected Reasoning Process

This problem requires:

1. **Calculate annual standard working hours** based on the given parameters
2. **Determine how to treat paid annual leave** - the question specifies it as "mandatory rest time" (必休时长)
3. **Account for both taken and untaken paid leave**
4. **Compare actual attendance with expected attendance**
5. **Arrive at a clear conclusion** about who owes whom, and how many hours

## Why This Is Challenging

The problem involves multiple interpretations:

- Should we use 21.75 days/month (payroll base) or actual working days (250 days/year)?
- How should "mandatory rest time" be interpreted?
- Should untaken paid leave be counted as work time or rest time?
- What is the baseline for comparison?

These ambiguities led to dramatically different answers across models.
